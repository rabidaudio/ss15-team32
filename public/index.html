Quick Comments

A drop-in comments framework that won't slow you down.

Customizable

Designed to match your design

Your comments should look like they belong on your site. Blocks ship with only the styles they need to function. Not a designer? See the examples for some added styling suggestions.

Small

5.3KB minified and gziped

Your site shouldn't be weighed down by comments. They load quickly and update live. If you're already using Firebase, there are no extra dependencies or requests to make.

In House

Keep ownership of your data

By storing data on your own site, you're retaining control of user's data and privacy. Use that data to power other parts of your app, and reduce unnecessary third-party tracking. In a future release, you'll even be able to use your existing site authentication.

Image from Jeff Sheldon via Unspash.

Drop-in

First add this tag where you want your comments block to go:

<qcomment></qcomment>

Then, add this with your scripts:

<script src="http://ss15-team32.divshot.io/lib/quick-comments.min.js"></script>
<script>
  new QC({
    firebase: new Firebase('https://<YOUR-FIREBASE-SITE>.firebaseio.com')
  });
</script>

Voila! See the docs for more.

Bower

bower install quick-comments
